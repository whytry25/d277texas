Some info about Austin(The capitol of Texas)

The population of Austin Texas is 974,447. Currently Austin Texas is one of the fastest growing cities in America. This is in part due to the high tech demand and of course Elon Musk's decision to move the TESLA company here.

Austin was incorporated into Texas in the year 1839. The backstory on this is that the independent Republic of Texas chose a tiny village, formerly called Waterloo located near the Colorado river at the time. This was later renamed to Austin because of Stephen F. Austin.

Austin Texas is located in the central part of Texas. This location also happens to put it in the Texas hill country. meaning that there are many lakes and rivers and trails to explore in the area.

the City of Austin Texas is known as a suburban area. The city is sprawling with new and beautiful suburban developments. This is in part due to the booming economy from again, the tech industry. When I say sprawling I mean rapid development at that. The most luxurious ones though, are the ones that reside on the river front.

Income is a very tricky subject when it comes to Austin Texas. This is in part due to their booming economy. The average household income, assuming there's two people, is $122,474. This number is very high, much like something you would see in cities like LA or Chicago. The median household income comes out to $86,000 or somewhere around there depending on the year.

This income level greatly blows out the average Texas household income sitting at $102,000. That means that on average each family in the city of Austin gets at least $20,000 more spending money compared to the rest of the state to do whatever they want with. This is like getting a part time job except its built into you salary.

There are three reasons one would move to the capitol of Texas. Those being:

  • Beautiful city life.
  • endless opportunities specifically in the tech field.
  • Scenery as in the landscape around you.
A fun fact about Austin is that it is what's considered the Music Capitol of the World with over 200 live music venues and over 2,000 musicians!
